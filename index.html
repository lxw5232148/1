    • Long Commute Time

    People are a far way to the workplace, so if there are two things to care about, work and raise children, people often choose raise children, and then choose the mother or father to give up their work to raise children, the answer always to be the mother.

    Built for the Problem

    Long-term commuting is often because the place of residence has a long distance from where the work gathers. People need to be able to reach it through various modes of transportation, but if the house, work space, place to raise children, and other public spaces are vertical The direction is dispersed to form a vertical settlement, and the traffic is transported to each floor through a high-speed vertical elevator, which will greatly reduce the commuting distance and work and take care of the children.

    Rendering

    • People gathering in this building, living, working and raising children together.
    It saves time in commute in the road surface, and easy to get to the place people want to go, just use the elevator and other health travel way such as bicycle and on foot, without a car.

    Function zoning

    • The building has an elliptical plane, which is a schematic diagram of the division of the functional area, but it can be arbitrarily divided according to the needs of use.
    There are 16 elevators evenly distributed in the middle, and the outer circle is the landscape and the area of childcare.
    In this way, people's daily lives are condensed together.

    Magnetically Levitating Elevators

    The key point in the realization of such a building that shortens the commute distance is that a high-speed elevator, magnetically levitating elevators, which is just such a high-speed elevator.
    This elevators "use much less floor space than traditional elevators, since multiple cars can operate on the same track. "
    And the elevator has more choices in the direction, no longer a single vertical up and down. "there are no limitations when it comes to height; the maglev elevators can go two miles high if necessary. And if an architect is interested in making a building that is particularly wide, the elevators can act as mini-trains, transporting passengers horizontally as well. It's even possible for the elevators to travel horizontally and then “change gears” to move upward or downward. The rail carrying the elevator simply rotates to switch the car's direction."
    High speed shortens the time people use to switch locations, and changes in direction also enable people to reach their destinations at higher speeds.

    Mixed-use development

    Definition
    "1) provides three or more significant revenue-producing uses (such as retail/entertainment, office, residential, hotel, and/or civic/cultural/recreation)
    2) fosters integration, density, and compatibility of land uses
    3) creates a walkable community with uninterrupted pedestrian connections."
    Vertical Mixed-Use Development
    "Combines different uses within the same building
    Provides for more public uses on the lower floor such as retail shops, restaurants, of commercial businesses
    Provides for more private uses on the upper floors such as residential units, hotel rooms, or office space."
    In the future development, urban planning and architectural design will be more infiltrated and the planning method will be more flexible. Urban design on a vertical level gives people more possibilities to live and create more conditions for equal opportunities for men and women.
